🌦️Rain Policy:

Rain in the forecast? No problem! You can reschedule your rental at no extra charge with at least 24 hours notice.

🚸Occupancy Recommendations:

  • Maximum of 5-7 kids of similar size in a bounce house at one time.
  • One person at a time on each slide lane.

🛡️🧽Cleaning Standards:

All our inflatable units are thoroughly cleaned and sanitized before every rental. Feel confident your event is safe and fun!

✅ What’s Included:

  • Extension Cord
  • Inflatable Blower
  • Safety Stakes for grass setups (or sandbags for concrete/turf — select your surface at checkout)

📍What You’ll Need:

  • Enough clear space for us to set up your inflatable.
  • A dedicated electric outlet within 50–75 feet of the setup area.
  • For water slides: a water source 🌊 and hose within 50 feet of the setup area.
